## The 8 Fundamental Structures of the Generative Architecture

### 1. The Realm (Canvas)
*The absolute spatial bound of existence.*
* **Mereology:** Contains an array of mutually exclusive `Domains`.
* **Description:** The structural subdivider uses a **Tartan Grid** algorithm. It defines intersecting asymmetrical rows and columns (thick bands crossing thin bands) rather than naive quad-trees. This mimics architectural weaving, generating massive rectangular trunks and tiny interstitial squares natively.

### 2. The Domain (Cell / Bounding Box)
*A rigid Euclidean sector generated by the Tartan Grid.*
* **Mereology:** A child of the `Realm`. Contains exactly one `Limit` and one `Manifold`.
* **Relation:** Domains communicate via **Fuzzy Cellular Automata**. A ripped/torn Domain bleeds kinetic stress into its neighbors. Massive structural Domains resist this totally, while medium Domains absorb a tiny fraction, feeling "so little fuzzy" vibration. The rigid isolation is topological, but the kinetic energy is shared syntactically.

### 3. The Limit (Margin)
*The absolute repulsive walls of the Domain.*
* **Mereology:** Property of the `Domain`.
* **Description:** The invisible structural barrier preventing the internal mesh from bleeding into the neighboring whitespace.

### 4. The Manifold (The Cloth / 2D Grid)
*The physical continuous substance of the artwork.*
* **Mereology:** Exists inside a `Domain`. Composed of an $M \times N$ matrix of `Nodes` connected iteratively by `Tension Edges`.
* **Description:** A topological net representing woven fabric. It rests in a state of perfect parallel structural lines. Its topology can never be broken (it can tear wide open, but its strings cannot snap).

### 5. The Node (Particle)
*The fundamental atomic unit of the system.*
* **Mereology:** A constituent of the `Manifold`.
* **Description:** A point holding an `(x, y)` coordinate and velocity `(vx, vy)`. 

### 6. The Tension Edge (The Thread / Spring)
*The physical structural bond evaluating elasticity.*
* **Mereology:** The link between contiguous `Nodes` in the `Manifold`.
* **Elastic Equation:** Evaluated as a spring striving for structural homeostasis against sheer force.

### 7. The Stress Tensor (The Energy Field)
*The chaotic external reality deforming the substance.*
* **Description:** A continuous noise field imparting chaotic displacement onto the `Nodes`.
* **Relation:** Evaluated per Domain, scaled exactly by the Domain's Automata Vulnerability state.

### 8. The Ordering Law (The Tangle-Prevention)
*The topological mathematical supremacy overriding all physics.*
* **Description:** The law that explicitly solves the tangling flaw. No matter how violently the `Stress Tensor` hits the `Manifold`, the nodes mathematically cannot cross their neighbors.
* **Equation:** `Node[n].position < Node[n+1].position - Epsilon`. (The threads compress infinitely tightly against each other, but never overlap).
